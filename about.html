Personal interests

As much as we try, I believe it's impossible to truly seperate our professional and personal worlds, as they always seem to have an influence on each other. Below are some of the things that interest me in my spare time and inform how I approach my professional work.

Music

Where would the world be without music? It has guided me, comforted me, driven me and has been the soundtrack to my inner cinema for as long as I can remember. Music is a force within my life that has likely had the most profound effect on shaping who I am and how I feel. Ever since I discovered my sister's "alternative" music collection in early highschool, I've been continually exploring and discovering new music and sharing it to whoever would listen (not many, as it turns out).

Here is a list of some of my favourite albums:

  • Stolen Violin, Temperate Touch, Tropical Tears (2013)

  • King Krule, Man Alive! (2020)

  • Jordan Ireland With Purple Orchestra, Jordan Ireland With Purple Orchestra (2017)

  • Arthur Russell, World of Echo (1986)

  • Sunbeam Sound Machine, Wonderer (2014)

  • Amen Dunes, Love (2014)

  • Connan Mockasin, Forever Dolphin Love (2011)

  • Soda Eaves, Murray, Darling (2016)

Photography

I began my journey into photography as a young teenager who was drawn to the way certain objects, spaces or light met my eyes. I've always been enamoured by the ability that cameras have to capture moments in time.
